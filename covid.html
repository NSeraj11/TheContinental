Covid 19 update

Here at The Continental we value the health and safety of all our guests and employees. We would like to assure you that our staff goes above and beyond in sanitization of rooms, bathrooms, and common areas.

Starting March 7, 2022 the Key to NYC rules will be suspended. Indoor venues, including restaurants, fitness facilities, and entertainment spaces will no longer be required to check for proof of vaccination before customers enter. Additionally, masks are to be worn in all public places by unvaccinated guests and customers. For fully vaccinated customers and guests, masks are optional.

Customers arriving from outside of the United States should review government travel guidance to confirm eligibility and requirements for travel. See Travel.State.gov

From of all of us here at The Continental, we hope you feel at home while you stay with us!
